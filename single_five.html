Tahkuban

ROLE GRAPHIC DESIGNER

YEAR 2016

Home Work Details

Overview

Tahkuban is a niche clothing brand developed as part of my final year project, aimed at introducing a metalcore-themed apparel line to the Malaysian market. While the local market is saturated with hip hop-inspired brands, there was a clear opportunity to target the underserved metalcore audience. Drawing inspiration from Indonesia's successful ventures in this space, I focused on creating strong branding and design tailored to this niche. The brand was designed to cater to both Malay and English-speaking audiences, simplifying the metalcore culture for new fans and guiding them in discovering bands and styles. Following its launch in November 2016, Tahkuban achieved notable success, selling over 100 products within the first week.


Problem

While metalcore music has gained a loyal following in Malaysia, there was a noticeable absence of clothing brands tailored specifically to this genre. The local market was heavily saturated with hip hop-inspired apparel, leaving a gap for metalcore enthusiasts seeking fashion that reflected their musical preferences. This presented a missed business opportunity, as no brand effectively captured the interests of this growing niche. The challenge was to develop a brand that not only appeals to metalcore fans but also creates a connection between music and fashion, addressing an unmet demand in the Malaysian market.


Brand Identity

The logo design for Tahkuban incorporates bold, dynamic shapes that evoke feelings of love and care, while reflecting the energy and intensity of the metalcore community. This thoughtful combination of elements ensures the brand maintains a balance between its rebellious identity and an approachable, inclusive appeal, resonating with both seasoned fans and newcomers alike.

Clothing Design

The clothing design for Tahkuban reflects the bold and edgy aesthetic of the metalcore scene, combining dark tones with striking graphics that appeal to fans of the genre. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted to align with the rebellious yet authentic spirit of the brand. The designs feature a unique blend of style that stands out in the market while maintaining comfort and wearability for everyday use.
