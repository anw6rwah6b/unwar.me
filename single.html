Sunway PFM App

ROLE UI UX DESIGNER

YEAR 2021

Home Work Details

Background

Sunway Property & Facility Management Sdn. Bhd. (Sunway PFM) is a leading provider of comprehensive real estate solutions, established in 1991. there are a part of the Sunway Group, Malaysia’s largest multinational conglomerate known for excellence and sustainable development.

Goals

Creating an application that ensure social distancing due to COVID-19, improve communication between tenants, homeowners, and management through an app, allow residents to manage visitor access easily via the app.

My Impact

Enhanced user experience, improved communication, increased efficiency, ensured accessibility, built trust with secure design, and created a visually appealing app for better engagement and satisfaction.

Research

Goals

Below are the areas I’d like to explore during the user research. I want to understand:
  • Identify the specific requirements and pain points of residents, homeowners, and building management to design a user-centric app.
  • Gather insights on how users interact with the app to streamline navigation and enhance the overall user experience.
  • Determine the most effective ways to facilitate communication between tenants, homeowners, and management within the app.
  • Analyze current property management workflows to design features that save time and improve operational efficiency.

Methodologies

  • Observe users interacting with the app to identify usability issues and areas for improvement.
  • Test different design elements and features to determine which versions perform best with users.
  • Analyze app usage data to understand user behavior and identify trends.

Ideation

Feature Roadmap

When I got to the point of really understanding what the client needs, the time has arrived to brainstorm the potential feature that should be designed during this project timeline. Must Haves are the features that are feasible to design within the project timeframe.

Task & User Flows

The app map enabled the design of efficient user and task flows for the Sunway PFM app. Users begin by downloading the app, signing in, and accessing features like visitor registration, arrival/departure tracking, and notifications. The app's design ensures smooth, on-the-go use with intuitive navigation and push notifications.

Task: User wants to register a visit.

Lo-fi Wireframes

The next step in my process was creating low-fidelity wireframes for the Sunway PFM app. To build the complete task flow, I experimented with different layouts, button styles, and navigation options. I considered variations in button size and shape to optimize usability for features such as visitor registration and real-time notifications. I chose rounded, larger buttons to enhance visibility and interaction, ensuring the design supports seamless use, especially in a mobile context.

Iterations

The first version of lo-fi wireframes allowed me to run early tests and discover the priority revisions that need to be implemented before I start building the high fidelity prototype. The version below includes the revisions made to the information architecture and microcopy such as:

  • Enlarged and thickened icons to improve visibility and usability.
  • Made “Notifications” more accessible by repositioning them in the main navigation.
  • Updated the background content to enhance readability and user focus.

UI Kit

A key challenge of this project was to integrate new features seamlessly while preserving the Sunway PFM app's brand identity. To enhance both usability and aesthetics, I developed a comprehensive UI kit that included a mix of larger, clearer icons for primary actions and smaller, thinner icons for secondary functions. This balanced approach ensured a consistent interface, enabling users to navigate efficiently and intuitively access key features.

Long-term recommendations

  • Enhance user interaction with an intelligent chat assistant.
  • Integrate rewards, vouchers, and promotional deals to encourage user engagement.
  • Expand payment options to include e-wallets, online banking, and other digital methods.
  • Implement smart parking solutions, including online parking fee payments.
  • Develop a customizable news feed to provide relevant updates to users.
  • Include an events calendar to keep users informed of upcoming activities and gatherings.

Conclusion

The Sunway PFM app is designed to simplify daily interactions and enhance the overall experience for residents, homeowners, and building management. By incorporating essential features such as secure login, visitor management, and real-time notifications, the app aims to foster a connected community while optimizing operational efficiency. The inclusion of additional features like language options, smart parking, and payment integrations further adds value, offering convenience and flexibility to users.

Ultimately, the Sunway PFM app provides a comprehensive solution that not only meets the current needs of property management but also adapts to future demands, ensuring a high level of satisfaction and engagement among all stakeholders.
