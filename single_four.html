Overview

Eamart is a versatile e-commerce platform, offering a broad range of products across various categories. From household items to electronics, Eamart serves as a one-stop-shop for customers' diverse needs. One of its prominent sub-brands, Pet Global (formerly A.S. Frost), specializes in distributing premium dog and cat food, further expanding Eamart's portfolio into the pet care market. This combination of variety and specialization helps Eamart cater to a wide audience while maintaining its reputation for quality in niche sectors like pet nutrition.

Marketing Efforts

To enhance market penetration and engage a wider audience, we developed professionally designed promotional materials in the Malay language, targeting Malay-speaking customers and those proficient in the language. The promotional cards prominently feature our cat food products and are strategically positioned to maximize visibility and appeal. These materials are specifically aimed at branch managers of pet food retailers, with the objective of driving product adoption, increasing in-store promotions, and ultimately boosting overall sales across our distribution network.
