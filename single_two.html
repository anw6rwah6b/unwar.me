iSchool App

ROLE UI UX DESIGNER

YEAR 2020

Home Work Details

Background

iSchool app is an educational platform designed to facilitate effective communication between teachers, students, and parents. It streamlines school management by handling security and safety, teacher-student announcements, student homework, class timetables, student leave applications, and provides essential emergency contacts for a safer and more organized school environment.

Goals

Developing an application that enhances communication between teachers, students, and parents, streamlines school operations, allows easy management of student assignments and schedules, ensures safety through real-time updates, and provides a centralized platform for accessing important school information.

My Impact

Improved the user experience by creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces, streamlined communication between teachers, students, and parents, optimized workflows for increased efficiency, ensured accessibility for diverse users, implemented secure and reliable design systems, and crafted a visually appealing design to enhance overall engagement and user satisfaction.

Research

Goals

Below are the areas I’d like to explore during the user research. I want to understand:
  • Identify the specific needs and pain points of teachers, students, and parents to design a user-centered app.
  • Determine the most effective ways to facilitate communication between teachers, students, and parents within the app.
  • Analyze current school management workflows to design features that save time and improve operational efficiency.

Methodologies

  • Observe teachers, students, and parents interacting with the app to identify usability issues and areas for improvement.
  • Test various design elements and features to determine which versions offer the best user experience.
  • Analyze app usage data to understand user behavior and identify trends in communication, task management, and overall engagement.

Ideation

Feature Roadmap

After identifying the solutions that would bring the most value to the users, I decided to take it a step further and brainstorm the key features for the iSchool app. My goal was to create an easy-to-use platform that simplifies daily school management for teachers, students, and parents, whether they're at home or on the go. The app requires a user-friendly interface, streamlined onboarding, and quick access to essential features like homework management and communication.

Task & User Flows

Having a well-defined app structure enabled me to design the user and task flows for the iSchool app. The user flow assumes that parents are logged in and can seamlessly navigate key tasks. These tasks include checking their children's class schedules, attend classes, and submitting leave requests. The app is designed to be intuitive and efficient, allowing parents to complete tasks smoothly while staying informed of important events and updates.

Task: The user needs to check class schedules, attend classes, and submit a leave request.

Lo-fi Wireframes

The next step in my process was creating low-fidelity wireframes for the iSchool app. Before finalizing the design, I explored various layout options and button styles to ensure the app was user-friendly for parents. I focused on making the interface intuitive, with easily accessible features like checking schedules, attending classes, and submitting leave requests. The wireframes helped establish a clear task flow, ensuring a smooth and efficient user experience on all devices.

Iterations

The initial version of the low-fidelity wireframes provided valuable feedback, leading to several key improvements in the design. These iterations ensured that the app would offer a better user experience for parents:

  • To enhance usability, the button sizes were increased, making it easier for parents to navigate the app and complete tasks like submitting leave requests.
  • The navigation flow was streamlined to reduce the number of steps needed to access key features such as class schedules and attending classes.
  • Adjustments were made to the overall layout to improve clarity and make key features, such as the class timetable and homework submissions, more accessible.

UI Kit

For the iSchool app, I developed a comprehensive UI kit to ensure consistency across all design elements. The kit included various button styles, icons, and typography choices that aligned with the app’s user-centric approach. I designed larger, easy-to-tap buttons to enhance usability for parents, ensuring key features like class schedules, leave requests, and homework management were easily accessible. Additionally, I incorporated color schemes and fonts that maintain a clean, professional look, while promoting a seamless and intuitive user experience.

Long-term recommendations

  • Implement a more advanced messaging system for real-time teacher-parent communication.
  • Introduce a parent dashboard with personalized updates and notifications about their child's progress.
  • Integrate a student performance tracker to monitor academic and extracurricular activities.
  • Enable integration with third-party educational platforms for extended learning resources.
  • Develop a feature for school event scheduling with reminders and RSVP options.

Conclusion

The development of the iSchool app has laid the foundation for a more streamlined and efficient school management system. By addressing key pain points for parents, such as managing class schedules, homework, and leave requests, the app simplifies daily tasks and improves communication between schools and families. With its user-friendly design and focus on accessibility, the iSchool app creates a more connected and engaging educational experience, paving the way for future enhancements that will further support the needs of parents, students, and educators.
